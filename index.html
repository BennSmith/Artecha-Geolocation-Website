About this Project

This project uses geolocation to map the locations of users across Miami's campus onto an image. As people move across campus, they will gradually reveal more and more of the image.

How to Participate
  1. Download the Expo app from the app store.
  2. Login to Expo with the following account
    User: geo-artecha
    Password: artecha
  3. In the Profile tab, under "Published Projects" click on IMS-Artecha
  4. Press the "BEGIN" button to recording location the app. You may now leave the app.
